🏦 Bobby's Bank — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What We Collect

Bobby's Bank collects and stores the following information:

  • Your Discord username and user ID
  • Your token balance and transaction history (deposits and withdrawals)
  • Timestamps of transactions
  • No-gamble lock settings if activated

2. What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your email address, password, payment information, messages, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Bobby's Bank token balance system — tracking deposits, withdrawals, and balances. It is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on the bot's host machine in a JSON file and optionally in a Google Sheet accessible only to the server administrator.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion of your data by contacting the server admin.

6. Third Parties

Bobby's Bank interacts with case-clicker.com to facilitate trades. We do not share your Discord data with case-clicker.com or any other third party.

7. Children

This service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept any changes.
